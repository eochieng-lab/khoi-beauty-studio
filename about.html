A Sanctuary for Your Beauty

Khoi Beauty Studio is more than a salon — it's a sanctuary where beauty meets artistry. Nestled in Lavington Business Hub, Nairobi, we offer a full suite of professional services in face & skin treatments, hair care, and nail artistry, each tailored to highlight your natural beauty.

Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or investing in your everyday self-care, our expert team delivers an elevated, luxurious experience that leaves you looking and feeling your absolute best.

Our mission is simple: to empower every client who walks through our doors to embrace their unique beauty with confidence. We combine world-class techniques with warm Kenyan hospitality, creating an experience that rejuvenates not just your appearance, but your spirit.
